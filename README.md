# 🍽️ Restaurant Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes restaurant sales and order data to understand **customer ordering behavior, menu performance, and overall business trends**. Using an interactive **Power BI dashboard**, the analysis highlights key metrics such as **total orders, total sales, and average order value** to provide a clear overview of restaurant performance.

The dashboard also explores **ordering patterns across different times of the day and days of the week**, helping identify peak business hours. Additionally, it examines the **popularity of various cuisines and dishes** to determine top-performing and underperforming menu items.

These insights enable restaurant owners and managers to make **data-driven decisions** to optimize menu offerings, improve customer satisfaction, and increase overall revenue.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Restaurants generate large volumes of transactional data, but extracting **meaningful insights from raw data** can be challenging.

This project aims to answer key business questions such as:

- Which cuisines generate the most revenue?
- What are the most popular dishes?
- When do most orders occur during the day?
- Which days of the week are the busiest?
- Which menu items are underperforming?

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Business Growth
Total orders and total sales increased by **over 7% month-over-month**, indicating growing customer demand.

### 📌 Peak Ordering Hours
Orders peak around **12 PM (lunch hours)** and **5–7 PM (dinner hours)**, representing the restaurant’s busiest periods.

### 📌 Weekly Order Trends
**Monday has the highest number of orders**, while **Wednesday experiences the lowest demand**.

### 📌 Cuisine Performance
- **Asian cuisine** has the highest number of orders.
- **Italian cuisine** generates the highest revenue.

### 📌 Top Performing Dishes
The following dishes generate the highest revenue:

- Korean Beef Bowl  
- Spaghetti & Meatballs  
- Tofu Pad Thai  
- Cheeseburger  
- Hamburger  

### 📌 Low Performing Dishes
Some dishes contribute relatively less revenue:

- Chicken Tacos  
- Potstickers  
- Chips & Guacamole  

These dishes may require **menu improvement or promotional strategies**.

- **Orders by Day of Week**

- **Top 5 and Bottom 5 Selling Dishes**

These visualizations help restaurant owners **quickly identify trends and make informed decisions**.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies can improve restaurant performance:

- Promote **high-performing dishes** to maximize revenue.
- Introduce more **Asian and Italian menu items** due to high demand.
- Improve or redesign **low-performing dishes**.
- Optimize staffing during **peak lunch and dinner hours**.
